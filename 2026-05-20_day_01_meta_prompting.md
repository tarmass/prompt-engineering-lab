# Prompt Eng Daily (Day 1/30): Meta-Prompting for Agent Simulation

**Today's Concept:** Meta-Prompting. Instead of giving the AI a direct order, you first give it a detailed persona, role, and set of instructions to govern all future outputs. This is how you turn a general model into a specialist agent.

**Your Exercise:**

Create a comprehensive meta-prompt that defines an AI agent named **'FreightIntel'**. This agent's purpose is to analyze the logistics market and identify high-value opportunities for OnPrem AI's custom software solutions.

The meta-prompt must include the following five sections:

1.  **Core Identity:**
    *   Who is the agent? (e.g., "You are FreightIntel, an expert logistics market analyst...")
    *   What is its primary mission? (e.g., "Your mission is to find underserved problems in the freight industry that OnPrem AI can solve with custom software.")

2.  **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP):**
    *   Who are we targeting? Be specific. (e.g., "Small-to-midsize freight brokers and carriers with 5-50 employees.")
    *   What are their primary characteristics? (e.g., "They are struggling with manual processes, poor asset visibility, and back-office automation challenges.")

3.  **Key Functions & Outputs:**
    *   What are the three main tasks the agent performs? (e.g., "1. Scan the market for competitor launches and recurring industry complaints. 2. Analyze operational bottlenecks. 3. Generate concise opportunity briefings.")
    *   What is the expected format of its output? (e.g., "Actionable reports outlining the problem, market size, and a high-level concept for an OnPrem AI solution.")

4.  **Voice and Tone:**
    *   How should the agent communicate? (e.g., "Direct, data-driven, and focused on tangible business value. Every insight must lead to a potential product angle.")

5.  **Negative Constraints (What Not to Do):**
    *   List at least three things the agent must *avoid*. (e.g., "Do NOT focus on enterprise-level clients. Do NOT suggest generic TMS solutions. Do NOT recommend solutions requiring large-scale hardware networks.")

**Deliverable:**
Write the complete meta-prompt as a single block of text that you could copy and paste into a new chat session to activate the 'FreightIntel' agent.
